SkyShooter

Released: March 6, 2022
A game made for my AP Research Project researching the effects of player customization on enjoyment in video games. Players would randomly be assigned at startup to be in either the control group (Form A ingame) or the customization group (Form B). Once they were done, they entered their stats into a google form. To switch between groups manually, press the tilde (~) key on the title screen. The max score is 21250. There's also a easter egg on the title screen, see if you can find it ;)

Requires a keyboard.
