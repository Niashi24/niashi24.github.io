Speed Pong

Releasing: Spring 2024

---Summary---
Speed Pong is a more modern, competitive version of the classic Pong game.

---Gameplay---
Standard pong game rules apply: Move your paddle to hit the ball back towards your opponent.

Unique to Speed Pong is the charge meter: by rallying the ball back and forth you will build up a charge that you can spend on a special move that increases the speed of the ball, allowing you to surprise your opponent and score.
